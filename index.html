About

T. Shan is a singer, rapper & songwriter based out of NYC, but his roots stretch far beyond. Raised between China and both coasts of the US, his music blends increasingly wide genre influences united by his biting, confessional lyrics. His music reflects the highs and lows of modern love, yet never loses sight of where he and his family came from.

T. Shan has amassed over 3.5 million streams across all platforms, and he’s been featured in Wonderland Magazine, 88Rising radio, and C-Heads Magazine, among others.

On his latest EP “Flowers & Spice,” released in 2023, T. Shan sought to explore a host of new sonic terrains, from indie rock ballads to house rap dance tracks, all while telling a love story from its blossoming to its post-mortem. Intimate and hectic, saccharine yet bitter, the record braids together reflections of diving headfirst into love while hesitant from the aches of past scars, numbing yourself amidst strobe lights and D&B, and coming to peace with the good and bad of a bygone affection.
